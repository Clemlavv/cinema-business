History of Cinema

Cinema was born at the end of the 19th century. Although animation dates back at least to the 17th century, with the "thaumatrope", it was not until 1891 that the first patent for the animation of photographic images appeared and the first silver camera was successfully manufactured. The collective show that could result from this was born a few months later.
